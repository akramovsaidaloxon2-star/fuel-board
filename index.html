🛠 Tugmalar

📅 Reportlar (snapshot)

  • Haftalik / Oylik + nom yozib 📅 Reportga saqlash — shu paytdagi board nusxasini saqlab qo'yadi.
  • 🔴 Live board dropdown'i — saqlangan reportni ochadi. U faqat o'qish uchun (tahrirlab bo'lmaydi), lekin Excel/PDF qilsa bo'ladi.
  • Live board'ga qaytish uchun dropdown'dan 🔴 Live board ni tanlang.

🧮 Avtomatik ustunlar

  • Estimated difference = Toll calculator − Given direction (avtomatik).
  • Extra mile = Directed mile − Dispatched mile (avtomatik).
  • Qolgan ustunlarni o'zingiz kiritasiz.

🚦 Status qoidasi

  • FOLLOWED → soft saving hisoblanadi (Est diff = Toll calc − Given dir).
  • NOT FOLLOWED / SKIPPED → Est diff avtomatik $0 bo'ladi (haydovchi yo'nalishni bajarmagani uchun tejash yo'q).

💰 Pastdagi hisob (TOTAL + soft saving)

  • 🟠 TOTAL — har bir ustunning yig'indisi (qator qo'shilsa avtomatik o'sadi).
  • Extra Miles ($) = jami Extra mile × 1.5 — ortiqcha millarning pul qiymati.
  • Expected soft saving = jami Estimated difference − Extra Miles ($) — toll yo'llardan chetlanib tejalgan sof pul.
  • Expected soft saving with unfollowed = soft saving − jami Charge (haydovchidan undirilgan summa ayriladi).
  • Hammasi live hisoblanadi — qatorni o'zgartirsangiz darrov yangilanadi.

Last known — parked, engine off

A truck only streams fuel while its engine is on. These units are parked, so the board shows their last known fuel (just like the Motive dashboard) with the reading age. They flip back to live the moment they start driving — no action needed.

Never reported fuel yet

No fuel reading captured since the board started. This list shrinks as trucks drive and report for the first time. Units that stay here after a full day of driving are the genuinely sensor-less ones (often #-prefixed retired trucks) — those are worth checking in Motive.
